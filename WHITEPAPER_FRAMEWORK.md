# Human Knowledge Foundation Project (人类知识地基计划)

## Whitepaper Framework

### I. Core Vision
* **From "Exploration" to "Systematic Mining"**: For thousands of years, human scientific discoveries have resembled treasure hunts—highly dependent on the accidental flashes of genius from rare individuals. The ultimate goal of this project is to use artificial intelligence technologies, such as Large Language Models (LLMs), to transform scientific discovery into a **systematic and engineered "bedrock mining" process**.
* **The Grand Map of Human Knowledge Foundations**: Systematically map and analyze all "fundamental assumptions" and "implicit assumptions" that support humanity's existing knowledge architecture (particularly in mathematics and natural sciences), building a computable and verifiable network of foundational knowledge.

### II. Philosophical & Theoretical Foundations
> **⚠️ Origin & Reader's Guide**: This section deeply integrates the research findings from our exploration of "The Nature of Intelligence." It is important to note that these ideas originated from intuition-driven personal exploration, carrying the boldness and potential lack of rigor of a "scout squad." We recommend that all contributors approach these original background materials with a **"gold-miner's mindset"**—filtering out the noise, capturing the sparks of intuition, and ultimately solidifying them through the rigorous system engineering of this project.

* **The Nature of Intelligence is Efficiency**: Intelligence is not a mysterious subjective feeling, but rather the "efficiency" itself of a system utilizing internal models to achieve the most optimal predictions and decisions under resource constraints.
* **Cognitive Hierarchies and Assumptions**: The human cognitive system is an edifice built layer by layer, from low-level data to high-level paradigms. Each layer is built upon specific "assumptions" (even instinctive, unverified intuitive assumptions). The lowest-level axioms and hypotheses form the bedrock of this edifice.
* **Conflict-Driven Reconstruction**: Paradigm shifts in science (such as relativity and quantum mechanics) are essentially processes where the system traces back to the "bedrock assumptions" and modifies them when irreconcilable conflicts arise in high-level cognition.
* **The Principle of Non-Exceptionalism & Multi-Model Consensus**: Knowledge has no anthropocentric barriers in abstract computational spaces. This project advocates for a **"Multi-Model Objective Consensus Mechanism"**—allowing Large Language Models of different vendors and architectures (such as GPT-4, Claude 3.5, Gemini 1.5, DeepSeek) to conduct independent reasoning and consensus verification on the same topic, thereby eliminating anthropocentrism and single-model biases.

### III. Core Methodology
* **Hierarchical Deconstruction of Knowledge (L0 - Ln)**:
    * Establish a strict hierarchical assumption model: from L0 (sensory and instinctual assumptions), L1 (basic logical assumptions), up to higher-order scientific axioms and implicit physical assumptions.
* **Systematic Extraction Pipeline**:
    1. **Corpus Input**: Input highly rigorous scientific/mathematical texts or original research papers.
    2. **AI-Assisted Reverse Deconstruction**: Use LLMs to perform reverse-deduction, asking: "What unstated assumptions does this inference rely on?"
    3. **Consensus & Mechanical Review**: Rely on cross-validation and multi-model verification to generate "Assumption Cards" containing sources, arguments, and dependencies.
* **Frozen Models & Token Table Expansion**:
    * Explore a novel "in-memory computing" approach to knowledge internalization: freeze the powerful base model network and only expand and fine-tune its Token Embeddings. This directly injects extracted atomic concepts into the low-level representation, achieving ultra-efficient knowledge absorption that surpasses the limitations of traditional RAG.

### IV. Project Phases & Roadmap
* **Phase 1: Genesis - Minimum Viable Product (MVP)**
    * Establish an open-source organization on GitHub.
    * Select 1-2 classic scientific domains with the most rigorous logic and clear boundaries (e.g., Euclidean geometry, classical mechanics).
    * Define a standardized format for "Assumption Cards" and complete the initial setup of the toolchain and multi-model consensus testing.
* **Phase 2: Expansion - Core Natural Sciences Network**
    * Expand to electromagnetism, early quantum theory, modern physics, and advanced mathematics.
    * Form the first version of the "Human Knowledge Bedrock Atlas" with clear dependency relationships.
* **Phase 3: Automation & Deep Water**
    * Introduce formal verification tools (such as Lean, Coq) and mathematical LLMs to verify logical completeness.
    * Develop automated assumption-detection Agents to actively search for unnoticed implicit contradictions in existing scientific systems.

### V. Community Collaboration & Ecology
* **Open-Source Driven**: Built entirely on open-source principles on GitHub, utilizing permissive licenses such as CC BY-SA 4.0 or MIT.
* **Division of Roles**:
    * **Architects**: Responsible for methodology iteration and top-level design.
    * **Prompt Engineers**: Design and optimize prompts for multi-model cross-extraction.
    * **Domain Experts**: Professionals in mathematics, physics, and other disciplines, responsible for the final human judgment and professional validation of AI-extracted bedrock assumptions.
    * **Developers**: Write automation scripts, Agent workflows, and interactive visualization maps.
* **Long-Term Vision**: This is not just a document repository; it is a cradle for the next-generation "AI Einstein." Once bedrock assumptions are fully digitized, future AI systems will be able to modify any bedrock brick, quickly calculating and potentially "deducing" new universal laws in a vast assumption space.
